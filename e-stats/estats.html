E-commerce during the pandemic








Why did E-commerce prospered more in the pandemic than regular shopping

Since the dawn of this pandemic,e-commerce played an immensily significant role in people's life. Since they couldn't reach out to the regular stores for supplies,e-commerce platforms such as Amazon were the only way to get resources such as groceries

Why people hesitated in the beginning to buys things from e-commerce platforms

This was so because of the fact that when the pandemic wasn't in existence, the quality of the products were much poorer and the prices were too high for the products, But as of now there is no choice but to trust and buy goods from the e-commerce platforms

How did E-commerce helped in stabalizing the downfall of global economy

After the pandemic started started the Global economy was at its downfall, the market started going down at an alarming rate most people accepted that after the pandemic the economy will drop to a rate that was never considered even to be imagined. But with people using e-commerce platforms the rate of the global market is stabalizing and hopefully would get to its average point.

Will E-commerce platforms takeover the market industry after the pandemic ends?

Yes AND no. People have started to realize that ordering things online is a lot convinient since you just have to press a few buttons and done, as simple as that. It will influence a lot of people to switch from regular shopping to online shopping. But there a few downfalls which may restrict people to use e-commerce platforms as there core source of items. online shopping takes a lot of time shipping whereas regular shopping is way more effective in terms of time comsumption. There more drawbacks like quality, difference in prices etc.
